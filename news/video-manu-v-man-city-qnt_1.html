The 61-year-old has been the German national coach for a hugely impressive 15 years during which time hes won the World Cup but not the Euros. This is his last chance to add this notch to his managerial bedpost, as it will be also for some of his ageing squad of stars such as Manuel Neuer, Mats Hummels and Thomas Muller. France, though, will provide sturdy opposition.
